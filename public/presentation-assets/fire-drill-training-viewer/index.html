TURNSTILE_GUARD
KEY NUMBER 30 IS USED TO BREAK DOWN THE TURNSTILES
TURN THE 2ND TO THE BOTTOM KEY FROM RUN TO MAINTENANCE MODE AS SHOW IN THE PICTURE
THE TURNSTILES THEN HAVE THE ABILITY TO BE COLLAPSED TO ALLOW FLOW
DURING THE ACTIVE FIRE ALARM THE TURNSTILES AUTOMATICALLY HAVE THE ABILITY TO BE COLLAPSED

PERIMETER_GUARD
THIS GATE OFF OF ALAMEDA CAN BE OPENED WITH THE MASTER KEY IF NEEDED DURING THE DRILL OR BEFORE IF WE ARE AWARE OF THE DRILL BEFOREHAND
THIS GATE NEEDS TO BE LOCKED AGAIN WHEN THE DRILL IS OVER
PEOPLE DO TEND TO LEAVE OUT THE EMERGENCY EXITS IN THAT AREA
THE START OF THE FIRE_SCENARIO
AT THE START OF A REAL FIRE OR FIRE DRILL YOU WILL HEAR A LOUD PATTERNED ALARM GOING OFF IN THE AFFECTED LOCATION
OCCASIONALLY THE LOBBY WILL BE INFORMED FIRST, THEY WILL THEN INFORM THE REST OF THE STAFF TO RESPOND
THE LOBBY IMMEDIATELY ASSIGNS THE GUARD STAFF THEIR POSITIONS
GUARD STAFF IS REQUIRED TO STOP WHAT THEY ARE DOING AND RESPOND
NORTH & SOUTH_GATE PROCEDURES
DURING AN EMERGENCY OR EMERGENCY DRILL NO ENTRY OTHER THAN EMERGENCY SERVICES ARE PERMITTED
ONE LANE MUST REMAIN FREE FOR EMERGENCY SERVICES
CONE OFF BOTH LANES UNTIL THE EMERGENCY SERVICES ARRIVE OR UNTIL THE DRILL COMPLETES
LOBBY SUPERVISOR MUST NOT REMOTELY OPEN THE NORTH GATE (EXCEPT EMERGENCY SERVICES)
NORTH & SOUTH_GATE PROCEDURES
LIKE THE EXAMPLE IN THIS IMAGE HAVE ONE LANE CONED OFF AND ONE SIDE READY FOR EMERGENCY PERSONNEL
CONES SHOULD BE INSIDE OF THE SOUTH GATE GUARD SHACK
HAVE ANY OTHER VEHICLES LEAVE OR MOVE AWAY FROM THE OPEN LANE
WAIT FOR SUPERVISION TO GIVE THE CLEAR BEFORE REMOVING THE CONES AND ALLOWING TRAFFIC TO FLOW
